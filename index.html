(About Me)

I am a dedicated leader in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (also known as "Mormon") in Marikina branch. I have been happily married to my wife, Angelica Dela Cruz Borilla, since September 10, 2013. We have a wonderful son named Marco Ephraim Borilla.

Throughout my life, I have faced many challenges and trials, but I have learned to overcome them through faith in Jesus Christ, hard work, and perseverance. These experiences have shaped me into the person I am today, and I am grateful for the lessons I have learned along the way.

이천십칠년

(2017)

We faced a great challenge in our life. My wife's pregnancy became sensitive, which forced us to return to the Philippines. Upon our return, we discovered that our relatives had stolen our belongings from our house. Because we knew that the expenses for the delivery would be high, we had to save a lot. We couldn't even buy basic household items. We experienced sleeping on cardboard boxes for 2 weeks before our friends from the church gave us a bed, a dining table, and an electric fan and received some help from our family. Because the house was left unattended for a long time, some parts of the ceiling rotted.

이천이십일

(2021)

After four years of hard work, perseverance, endurance, and fervent prayers and faith in God, and constant efforts to rise from challenges, we have witnessed God's love and blessings upon us.
